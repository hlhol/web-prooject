Mock Ups


Home

The Home page serves as the primary gateway for users, showcasing the school's mission and values. It has a sign-in section for user registration and access. It features an image slider that highlights key aspects of the school, creating an engaging visual experience. The layout is designed to be intuitive, allowing users to easily navigate to other essential sections such as Admissions, Community, News, and Our Story. This approach ensures that visitors can quickly find the information they need, promoting a positive user experience.

Admission

The Admissions page is structured to provide comprehensive information about the enrollment process. It includes sections detailing school fees, campus life, and application requirements. The design emphasizes clarity and accessibility, ensuring that prospective students and their families can easily understand the steps involved in applying. The use of organized tables for tuition information enhances readability, making it straightforward for users to compare fees across different grades.

Community

The Community page fosters a sense of belonging by connecting students, faculty, and parents. It features sections for news, events, and resources, encouraging interaction among stakeholders. The design promotes engagement through visually appealing layouts, creating a vibrant online community that supports collaboration and communication.

News

The News page effectively showcases the school's achievements, featuring a clean layout that highlights key successes through visually engaging images. Each image is accompanied by descriptive text, fostering a sense of pride within the community. Below the achievements, the embedded video provides an immersive look into daily life at the academy, enhancing user engagement. The video is styled to fit seamlessly within the page, ensuring that it complements the overall design without overwhelming the viewer. This multimedia element enhances user engagement by providing a dynamic representation of the school's vibrant environment.

Making Of

The Making Of page outlines the design and development process behind the website, detailing the rationale for each design decision. It features mock-ups for each page, illustrating their intended functionalities. The layout promotes transparency, allowing users to appreciate the effort involved in the website's creation. Additionally, a dedicated section lists the names of the creators, acknowledging their contributions. A table of links to various resources used during development is also included, providing users with easy access to the tools and references that informed the project and encouraging them to revisit the page.

Our Story

The Our Story page provides an insightful overview of the journey taken to create the Blue Sky Academy website. It highlights the key elements of the project, including the focus on various topics such as Admissions, Community, News, and the Making of the Website. The page is designed to engage visitors by sharing the challenges faced during the development process and the collaborative efforts of the team. This page features a structured layout that effectively communicates the project's narrative. Each section is clearly defined, allowing users to easily navigate through them. The design emphasizes clarity and accessibility, ensuring that the information is presented in a visually appealing manner. By showcasing the team's dedication and the skills acquired during the project, the Our Story page fosters a sense of connection and transparency with the audience.

Justification

Website Design and Mock-ups

Each page design has been carefully crafted with a user-centric approach, ensuring that it serves the needs of both students and parents while remaining visually engaging. The low-fidelity mock-ups focus on simplicity and ease of navigation, crucial for a smooth user experience. The layout of each page has been designed with the goal of making the website functional and easy to maintain.

Impact of Design and Choice of Colors

The color palette was chosen to evoke a sense of professionalism and trust, while also being visually engaging. Blue represents stability and reliability, which are core values of the institution. Orange, on the other hand, adds warmth and energy, encouraging user interaction. These colors work together to create a harmonious and aesthetically pleasing environment that aligns with the educational focus of the site.

Impact of HTML, CSS, and JS Code on Maintainability

The website is built with a focus on maintainability, utilizing semantic HTML and a consistent use of div elements to structure the layout. By encapsulating each section and component within div tags and with clear class and ID naming conventions we create a clear and organized codebase that enhances readability and simplifies future updates. This modular approach allows for easy adjustments to individual sections without affecting the overall layout.


CSS is structured to apply styles to these div elements efficiently, promoting reusability and reducing redundancy. JavaScript is used judiciously to enhance interactivity while keeping the codebase lightweight and efficient. This thoughtful approach to coding ensures that future developers can easily understand and modify the site, accommodating upgrades and new features without extensive rework.
