Achievements

Achieving First place for the Third Time

Blue Sky Academy School achieved first place at the national level in the Kingdom of Bahrain in the 2024 Schools Football Olympics for the third consecutive year. Parents praised the school’s role in nurturing students’ talents and providing the support needed to help them achieve their aspirations. A parent also highlighted the Physical Education Department’s efforts, commending their role in developing his son’s skills and advancing the students’ overall abilities.

Happiness and Pride Fill the School

Happiness and pride fill Blue Sky Academy as it celebrates achieving an “Excellent” rating in this year’s Quality Evaluation. To commemorate the occasion, the school organized a special event to create lasting memories. The principal expressed gratitude to all academy members, including teachers, supervisors, and students, for their tireless efforts and dedication, which made this outstanding achievement possible for the thrid time in a row.

Achieving the Golid Medal in Relay Race

The school extends its heartfelt congratulations to its champion students for winning the gold medal in the relay race and securing first place at the Gulf Cooperation Council level. We take immense pride in their exceptional efforts and fighting spirit that led them to victory, while commending their teamwork and mutual support. The school wishes them a bright future filled with more achievements, emphasizing that supporting students is highest priority.
